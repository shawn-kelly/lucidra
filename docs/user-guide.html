❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right platform for my organization?
Use our AI-powered organization analysis tool by clicking "🔍 Analyze My Organization" on the Organization Types page. The system will assess your team size, industry, growth stage, and strategic needs to recommend the optimal platform. You can also review the platform descriptions to make your own choice.
Can I switch between different platforms?
Yes! You can access any platform at any time. Use the main navigation to return to the dashboard, then select "🏢 Organization Types" to choose a different platform. Your progress and data are saved across sessions.
Are the platforms mobile-friendly?
Absolutely! All platforms are designed with mobile-first principles and are fully responsive. The Micro-Organization platform is especially optimized for mobile use, with touch-friendly interfaces and streamlined workflows.
How do the strategic frameworks integrate with my existing data?
Lucidra includes a comprehensive integration framework that connects organizational data across all strategic tools. You can import data via JSON/CSV, and the system will automatically populate relevant metrics and recommendations across frameworks.
What makes Lucidra different from other strategic planning tools?
Lucidra is the only platform offering organization-type-specific strategic intelligence. Instead of one-size-fits-all tools, we provide specialized platforms optimized for your specific context, whether you're a startup seeking investment or a government agency improving public services.
Is my data secure on the platform?
Yes. All data is stored locally in your browser using secure localStorage. No sensitive information is transmitted to external servers. The platform works entirely client-side, ensuring your strategic information remains private and under your control.
